Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platform and services

How do I list my food business on your platform? ▼

You can register your food business by clicking the "Get Started" button on the homepage and filling out your business details. Our team will verify and activate your listing.

Can I receive orders through WhatsApp? ▼

Yes! With our Professional and Enterprise plans, you can enable WhatsApp order alerts and communicate directly with your customers.

What payment options are supported? ▼

We support all major UPI apps, credit/debit cards, and wallets. You can also choose to enable cash on delivery for local orders.

Is there a free trial available? ▼

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial with the Starter plan features so you can explore our platform risk-free before subscribing.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? ▼

Absolutely. You can manage your subscription from your dashboard and change your plan at any time based on your business needs.
